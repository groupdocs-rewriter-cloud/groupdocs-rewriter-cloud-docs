GroupDocs.Rewriter Cloud can process content for paraphrasing, summarization and synonymization in **11** languages:

- Arabic
- English
- French
- German
- Indonesian
- Italian
- Portuguese
- Russian
- Slovak
- Spanish
- Ukrainian

Paraphrasing detection and text simplification work only with textual content in English.
